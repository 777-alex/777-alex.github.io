About Me, Alex

Hello there! I'm Alex, a Graphic Designer based out of the Washington D.C. area. I graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a BFA in Graphic Design. Ever since I began interning in my sophomore year of college, design always has revolved around the audience for me. The reason for that is simple: I am not designing for myself 99% of the time, at the end of the day, I am creating something for you; The person reading this, the person who will see the final product and interact with it in some form.

Design to me means being able to create the experience for consumers. Create for them and always keep them in mind. My process for design is research, ideate, test, refine, and repeat.
