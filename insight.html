Insight

Insight is the result of a 16-week independent study, where I chose to look into Electronic Medical Records (EMR) and how to make them more accessible. Part of my intent was to have more accessibility between patients and clinicians, and allowing clinicians to also be able to manage their current appointments and see their schedule in real-time. As the project currently stands, I have mocked up the Clinician-facing side of this project, and still will be working on what the Patient-facing side. Using research done by others in medical studies, and looking into the guidelines and requirements for EHRs, I developed what is being presented below.
